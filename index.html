About this map. This organizer synthesizes widely cited frameworks (perception/attention hierarchies; executive‑function literature; dual‑process views of reasoning/decision; Global Workspace accounts of conscious access; predictive‑processing accounts of perception/action) and standard cognitive‑psychology taxonomies. Tiers are a teaching aid: “higher‑order” is shown as a subset of high‑level (maximally integrative, cross‑domain).
